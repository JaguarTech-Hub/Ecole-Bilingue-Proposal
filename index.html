Social Media Marketing Proposal

Prepared for: Ecole Bilingue Edulingua

New Achimota, Accra-Ghana

Website: www.ecolebilingueghana.com

1. Introduction

JaguarTech Hub is excited to present a tailored social media marketing proposal to enhance the online presence of Ecole Bilingue Edulingua. Our goal is to leverage your existing flyers to create a quick, impactful campaign that drives engagement, increases enrollment for the summer camp, and promotes the school's trilingual programs.

2. Objectives

  • Boost visibility for the French Summer Camp (Accra and London) and admissions for the trilingual Montessori homeschool
  • Increase followers and engagement on Instagram (@ecoleedulingua) and Facebook (Ecole Bilingue Edulingua)
  • Drive traffic to the school's website for inquiries and registrations
  • Generate at least 50 qualified leads for summer camp enrollment

3. Proposed Campaign Strategy

A. Platform-Specific Approach

Instagram (@ecoleedulingua):

  • Eye-catching carousel posts highlighting camp activities (coding, gymnastics, language classes) and testimonials
  • Reels showcasing student experiences, snippets of language lessons, and behind-the-scenes camp prep
  • Stories with polls (e.g., "Which language does your child want to learn? French/Spanish?"), countdowns to registration deadlines, and swipe-up links to the website
  • IGTV videos featuring teacher interviews and campus tours

Facebook (Ecole Bilingue Edulingua):

  • Detailed posts about camp benefits (e.g., "Why a trilingual summer camp?") with flyer visuals
  • Live Q&A sessions with staff to address parent queries
  • Targeted ads focusing on parents in Accra (for Accra camp) and diaspora communities (for London camp)
  • Facebook Event creation for summer camp with regular updates

B. Content Plan

Week Content Focus Post Types
Week 1 Campaign launch with flyer visuals and video teaser: "Give your child a summer of fun & learning!" Carousel, Reel, Story
Week 2 Feature testimonials from past students/parents and highlight curriculum benefits Video testimonials, Image quotes
Week 3 Highlight London camp perks (sightseeing, cultural immersion) with user-generated content contest: "Tag a friend who'd love this!" Reel, Story Challenge, Live Q&A
Week 4 Urgency-driven posts: "Limited spots left! Register now!" and enrollment success stories Countdown Story, Testimonial, Final Call-to-Action

C. Hashtag Strategy

Primary hashtags: #EcoleBilingueSummer #TrilingualKids #AccraToLondon #LearnFrenchWithUs #EdulinguaCamp

Secondary hashtags: #GhanaEducation #BilingualKids #MontessoriGhana #AccraParents #SummerCampGH

D. Influencer Collaboration

Partner with 3-5 parenting influencers in Ghana to share camp details with their audiences. Proposed budget: GHS 1,500-3,000 per influencer depending on reach.
